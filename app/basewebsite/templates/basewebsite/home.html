About Me

Welcome to my world. I am a techie living in south India. I have over 18 years of experience as a software developer responsible for building large scale web applications and data analytics platforms. I have also managed large teams across multiple timezones for large multinational companies. I thrive on solving new problems and am always looking for new technologies to learn.
